Smoothie Recipes

Welcome to my collection of delicious and healthy smoothie recipes. Enjoy!!!

Classic

  • 2 small bananas, broken into chunks
  • 1 cup frozen unsweetened strawberries
  • 1 (8 ounce) container vanilla low-fat yogurt
  • 3/4 cup milk

Purple Monster

  • 2 frozen bananas
  • 1/2 cup frozen blueberries
  • 1 cup orange juice
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)

Peanut Butter Banana

  • 2 bananas, broken into chunks
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons honey, or to taste
  • 2 cups ice cubes

Banana-Berry

  • 1 small banana
  • 6 oz. pineapple juice
  • 0.50 cup ice
  • 6 oz. blueberries
  • 6 oz. raspberries or blackberries
  • 2 tsp. honey
  • 1 tsp. grated peeled fresh ginger

Irresistible Peach

  • 2 cup peaches
  • 0.50 cup milk
  • 1 cup Ice cream
  • 1 tbsp. lemon juice
  • peach slices

Kiwi Strawberry

  • 1 banana
  • 6 strawberries
  • 1 kiwi
  • 1/2 cup vanilla frozen yogurt
  • 3/4 cup pineapple and orange juice blend

Chocolate Banana

  • 1 banana
  • 1 tablespoon cocolate syrup
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup crushed ice

Feel Good

  • 1/2 cup nonfat milk
  • 1/2 cup fat-free plain yogurt
  • 1/2 frozen banana, peeled and chopped
  • 2 tablespoons powdered protein supplement
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons flax seed
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1/2 cup frozen strawberries

Mango Lassi II

  • 1 cup ice
  • 2 mangos - peeled, seeded and diced
  • 2 cups plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
